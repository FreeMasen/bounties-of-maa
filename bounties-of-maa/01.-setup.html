1. Setup

Professor Tizzik has made a huge breakthrough in their research and is looking for spelljammers to test out an experimental vessel. A call has been sent out to the citizens of Revon to recruit brave souls to embark on an incredible adventure to far away worlds. Characters must have the Spell-casting ability to qualify, (Barbarian, Fighter, or Rogue characters must take a subclass to participate) and no Spelljamming experience is required! Volunteers will be paid 100gp after 4 weeks of participation. All participants in this study will be required to live at the research facility and all meals will be provided.

An Experimental Spelljamming Ship

The very first Grasshopper Ship was test piloted by Timmon Fizbang (assistant to Professor Tizzik). These early test flights were largely terrestrial. Using this rudimentary version, Timmon was able to successfully travel from Palicona to Duultha to retrieve a message for Professor Tizzik which took roughly a 6 hours to complete the round trip, a sea ship would have take a full day just to make one leg of this journey. After this incredibly achievement, the next test flight was to attempt to reach the moon around Maa. Upon exiting the atmosphere, a Squid ship attacked, since the pilot's concentration was occupied by the spelljamming helm, Timmon wasn't able to cast any spells or control the Auto Bolt Cannon to provide any resistance. The squid ship closed in quickly but Timmon was able to cast dimension door before the attack was able to land a final blow.

Professor Tizzik now having some credible and compelling evidence that spelljamming can be made more accessible by shrinking the size of the vessels, went in search of funding to build a second generation of ships. Viceroy Florian Ott was quite impressed with the potential and offered to invest in the research. The manufacture of 5 new ships took the better part of a year, and now the professor is looking for new test pilots, Timmon is understandably rattled by their encounter with the squid ship.

Many folks have attended the informational sessions but so far no one has signed up after learning of the risks. The investment made by Viceroy Florian Ott was with public funds provided by the Council of Stewards's Committee for Innovation in Magic (CIM) and the report for effectiveness of the investment is coming due, which has caused the Viceroy to put some extra pressure on the Professor to find some damn pilots.

Timmon Fizbang has recently been able to bottle a batch of Tonics of Interrupted Death, the team has reached an agreement with the morgue of Duultha to feed this tonic to those who have died a heroic death (meaning they are Heros an not common folk but the death can be up to the specifics of the death are up to the player). They have requested only those who have some kind of spellcasting ability to serve as the test pilots.

To address the need for the pilot to be concentrating on steering the spelljamming helm, Professor Tizzik has purchased the materials required to create a handful of Circlets of Concentration to attach to the helm of each Grasshopper Ship. Unfortunately, the materials were stolen in transit. Your first mission will be to recover these materials, however we need some training first.
